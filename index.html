About Scoreboard

Scoreboard provides insight into the OpenStreetMap ecosystem, making users more informed & more effective.

Campaigns

Scoreboard provides an overview of what’s happening on the system as well as detailed information on individual mappers, teams, campaigns, and countries.

Mappers

For mappers, Scoreboard provides motivation. Through their profile page, Scoreboard keeps track of users edits, awarding them badges as they progress.
